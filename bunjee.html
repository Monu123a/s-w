Elegilibity

Are You Ready to Take the Leap? Bungee jumping is an exhilarating adventure, but safety always comes first. Here are the eligibility requirements to ensure your jump is as safe and enjoyable as possible: Age Requirements Minimum Age: Participants must be at least 12 years old (parental consent required for minors). Maximum Age: No limit, as long as you’re physically fit and meet other requirements. Weight Requirements Minimum Weight: 40 kg (88 lbs) Maximum Weight: 120 kg (265 lbs) These limits may vary depending on the jump site. Health Considerations Participants must be in good health and free from: Heart conditions or high blood pressure Neurological disorders Recent surgeries or injuries Pregnancy If you’re unsure, consult your doctor before booking. Clothing and Footwear Wear comfortable, secure clothing (no loose items). Closed-toe shoes are mandatory for safety. Important Notes Participants must sign a waiver before the jump. Intoxicated individuals will not be allowed to jump. On-site instructors have the final say regarding a participant's eligibility.

Location

Where Can You Experience Bungee Jumping? Bungee jumping takes place in some of the most iconic and breathtaking locations around the world. From towering bridges to scenic cliffs and urban skyscrapers, each spot offers a unique combination of adrenaline and awe-inspiring views. Bridges with a View Feel the thrill as you dive from world-famous bridges: Kawarau Bridge, New Zealand – The birthplace of modern bungee jumping. Bloukrans Bridge, South Africa – The highest bridge bungee jump in the world. Royal Gorge Bridge, USA – Incredible views of the Arkansas River. Nature’s Marvels Jump into the heart of nature and take in breathtaking landscapes: Victoria Falls, Africa – One of the world’s seven natural wonders. Rishikesh, India – Jump amidst the serene backdrop of the Himalayas. Verzasca Dam, Switzerland – A dramatic jump seen in GoldenEye. Urban Adventures For a thrilling jump in the heart of the city: Macau Tower, China – One of the tallest bungee jumps globally. KL Tower, Malaysia – An urban escape with incredible cityscapes. Dubai, UAE – Crane bungee jumping over the vibrant city skyline. Custom Platforms Adventure parks and resorts worldwide offer safe and exhilarating jumps from purpose-built platforms.
